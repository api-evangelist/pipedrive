Social CRM tools combine traditional CRM systems with social media data to help businesses track and manage customer interactions across platforms. The article examines three key solutions — Pipedrive, Hootsuite, and Sprout Social — explaining how each enables sales teams to identify prospects through social listening, gain deeper customer insights, and convert engagement into revenue opportunities. These platforms centralize scattered social conversations, allowing teams to respond with context and improve customer experiences.
